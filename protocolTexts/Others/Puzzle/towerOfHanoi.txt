The Tower of Hanoi puzzle, there are three diamond posts. The first one is surrounded by sixty-four golden disks of increasing size from the top to the bottom. The monks are committed to move them from one post to another respecting two rules: only a disk can be moved at a time, and they can only be laid either on a bigger disk or on the floor. Their objective is to move all of them to the third post.

--test case from the Maude Manual
